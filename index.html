Wedding Dress Shopping Timeline & Plan

To find the perfect wedding dress, planning ahead is key. Ideally, you should start shopping 9-12 months before your wedding to allow enough time for ordering, alterations, and final fitting
  1. (2+ Months Before the Wedding) - Set a Budget. Decide on your price range, including alterations and accessories. Research & Find Inspiration.Browse Pinterest, bridal magazines, and designers to define your style. Create a Shopping List. List must-haves (silhouette, fabric, neckline, etc.). Your Shopping Crew. Choose 2-3 supportive people to accompany you.
  2. (9-12 Months Before the Wedding) - Bridal Appointments. Visit boutiques or bridal salons. Try different styles to see what suits you best. Yes to the Dress. Once you find “the one,” order it! Wedding dresses can take 6-9 months to arrive
  3. (6-8 Months Before the Wedding) - Shop for Accessories. Buy shoes, veil, jewelry, and undergarments for your fittings. Arrival Date. Check with the boutique about shipping timelines.
  4. (3-4 Months Before the Wedding) - First Fitting. Ensure the dress fits properly. Bring shoes and accessories. For Alterations. Most dresses need at least 2-3 rounds of alterations.
  5. (1-2 Months Before the Wedding) - Second Fitting. Check alterations and make final adjustments. Break in Your Shoes. Wear them at home to avoid discomfort on the big day.
  6. (3-4 Months Before the Wedding) - Final Fitting & Pick Up Dress. Ensure the dress fits perfectly. Steam & Store Properly – Keep it safe and wrinkle-free.
