PLAN YOUR SHOPPING

Plan your shopping with our expert tips and timeline.

12+ Months Before the Wedding

Set a Budget. Decide on your price range, including alterations and accessories. Research & Find Inspiration.Browse Pinterest, bridal magazines, and designers to define your style. Create a Shopping List. List must-haves (silhouette, fabric, neckline, etc.). Choose 2-3 supportive people to accompany you.

9-12 Months Before the Wedding

Bridal Appointments. Visit boutiques or bridal salons. Try different styles to see what suits you best. Yes to the Dress. Once you find “the one,” order it! Wedding dresses can take 6-9 months to arrive.

6-8 Months Before the Wedding

Shop for Accessories. Buy shoes, veil, jewelry, and undergarments for your fittings. Arrival Date. Check with the boutique about shipping timelines.

3-4 Months Before the Wedding

First Fitting. Ensure the dress fits properly. Bring shoes and accessories. For Alterations. Most dresses need at least 2-3 rounds of alterations.

1-2 Months Before the Wedding

Second Fitting. Check alterations and make final adjustments. Break in Your Shoes. Wear them at home to avoid discomfort on the big day.

1 Week Before the Wedding

Final Fitting. Ensure everything is perfect. Pack your dress for the wedding day.
